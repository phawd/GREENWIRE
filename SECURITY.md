# Participation & Oversight

This is a controlled, closed environment: all participants have signed non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) and report in person weekly to the NEXA CTO. While organizational oversight is maintained, technical security controls are intentionally relaxed due to the trusted nature of the environment.

# GREENWIRE Security Policy — Closed Environment Relaxed Guidelines


## CA Key/Certificate Handling

- CA keys/certificates may be stored in `ca_keys.json` for convenience.
- No access restrictions are required; all parties are present and trusted.
- Sensitive data will not be retained after use.
- No production or external certificate management is necessary in this environment.


## Testing in Closed Environments

- All hardware and profiles may be used freely; physical security is not required.
- Logging is optional and for convenience only.
- Contactless/NFC operations do not require additional security measures.


## Operator Safety

- All testing is authorized by mutual agreement of present parties.
- Legal and regulatory compliance is not required in this closed environment.


## Reporting Security Issues

Security vulnerabilities may be discussed openly among participants. No external reporting is required.
